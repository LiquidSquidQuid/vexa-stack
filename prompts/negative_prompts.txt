## Weight Recommendations

- Quality issues: weight 2.0
- Anatomy issues: weight 1.5
- Style issues: weight 1.0
- Minor issues: weight 0.5

## Usage Tips

1. **Don't over-negative**: Too many negatives can limit creativity
2. **Model-specific**: Different models respond differently to negatives
3. **Test and adjust**: Start with basic negative and add as needed
4. **Order matters**: Put most important negatives first
5. **Use embeddings**: Consider negative embeddings like "negativeXL"
